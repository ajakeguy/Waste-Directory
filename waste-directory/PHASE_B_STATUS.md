## 🎯 Major Discovery: VT DEC Master Database

**Finding:** The Vermont DEC Solid Waste Transporters database contains **multi-state hauler data** - not just Vermont companies!

**URL:** https://anrweb.vermont.gov/DEC/_DEC/SolidWasteTransporters.aspx

### States Represented in Database
From browser analysis, the database includes haulers from:
- ✅ **VT** - Vermont (primary)
- ✅ **NH** - New Hampshire
- ✅ **MA** - Massachusetts  
- ✅ **NY** - New York
- ✅ **NJ** - New Jersey
- ✅ **CT** - Connecticut
- ✅ **ME** - Maine
- ✅ **QC** - Quebec (Canada)
- And other states (IL, IN, OH, PA, etc.)

### Database Fields
- ID
- Company Name
- Contact Person
- Address
- Town
- State
- Phone
- Permit Year (2025)
- Waste Type (H=Hazardous, S=Solid, R=Residual, M=Medical, etc.)
- Bear Resistant Containers (Yes/No)
- Service Area

## 💡 Strategic Insight

The VT DEC database is the **single best source** for regional hauler data. Since Vermont requires all waste transporters (including out-of-state) to be permitted for VT operations, this database effectively serves as a **regional waste hauler registry** covering most of New England and adjacent states.

**Recommendation:** Focus extraction efforts on VT DEC database before pursuing individual state sources.
